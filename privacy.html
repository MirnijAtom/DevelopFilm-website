Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the DevelopFilm iOS app and this website handle your information.

  • No account, no analytics, no ads.
  • Journal data and photos stay on your device. They are not uploaded to our servers.
  • Payments are processed by Apple. We don’t receive your card details.
  • Contact-only data. If you email us, we use your email solely to reply and support you.

Data We Process

  • App data, on device only: journal entries, development settings, and photos are stored locally on your iPhone or iPad. We do not collect or receive this content.
  • Purchase data: Apple provides us with non sensitive transaction status, for example whether a subscription is active. We do not receive full payment details.
  • Support emails: if you contact us, we process your email and message contents to respond.

Do You Share or Sell Data?

No. We don’t sell personal data. We don’t share it with advertisers or data brokers.

Retention

  • On device journal data: remains until you delete the app or remove entries and photos within the app.
  • Support emails: kept as long as reasonably necessary to resolve and document support matters.
  • Purchase records: retained per statutory accounting and tax requirements, based on Apple settlement information.

Cookies and Tracking

We don’t use cookies or analytics on this site, and the app has no third party trackers.
